# About

## Project origins

The LARA (Lab Automation Research Architecture) suite was originally conceived by
**Mark Doerr** at the University of Greifswald, Germany.
**Stefan Maak** (University of Greifswald / Uppsala University) developed the lab scheduler
and is the primary maintainer of the core packages:
`uuPythonlab`, `uulaborchestrator`, `uulabscheduler`, and related components.
